Welcome to SharkHTML5!

The Way of playing with Coding

  • Main Page

Welcome to my teeny website about clever strategies and dirty tricks for creating awesome websites! The website is organised around the lessons I've learned from building websites for different purposes. Instead of being educational, the content is troubleshooting-focused and emphasises the practical aspects.


Enjoy!

Najam Alam Khan
